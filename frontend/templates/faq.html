Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OrOeye

What is OrOeye?

OrOeye is an AI-powered diagnostic tool that uses deep learning to detect oral cancer from histopathology images. It helps medical professionals and researchers identify potential cancerous tissues quickly and accurately.

How accurate is the AI model?

Our InceptionResNetV2 model achieves 98.2% validation accuracy. However, this is a screening tool and should not replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult with a licensed healthcare provider.

What kind of images can I upload?

The system accepts histopathology (microscopic tissue) images in common formats like JPG, PNG, and JPEG. For best results, use high-quality images with clear tissue structure.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All uploaded images are processed locally and not stored permanently. We prioritize your privacy and follow best practices for medical data handling. Logged-in users' analysis history is encrypted.

What is Grad-CAM?

Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) is a visualization technique that shows which parts of the image the AI model focused on when making its prediction. This helps make the AI's decision-making process more transparent.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can use the analysis tool without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your analysis history, download detailed PDF reports, and track your scans over time.

Can this replace a doctor's diagnosis?

No. OrOeye is a screening and research tool, not a medical device. It should be used to assist medical professionals, not replace them. Always seek professional medical advice for definitive diagnosis and treatment.

How was the model trained?

The model uses transfer learning with InceptionResNetV2 pre-trained on ImageNet, then fine-tuned on over 10,000 oral histopathology images. Visit our Research page for more technical details.
